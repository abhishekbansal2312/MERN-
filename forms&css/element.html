Attribute Selectors Example

This paragraph has a custom data attribute and will have a lightgray background.

This paragraph has data-type="primary" and will be blue and bold.

This paragraph has data-type="secondary" and will not be styled by the data-type="primary" selector.

This paragraph has data-tags containing "important" and will have a red border.

This paragraph has data-tags not containing "important" and will not have a red border.

This paragraph has data-lang="en-US" and will be italic.

This paragraph has data-lang="en-GB" and will be italic.

This paragraph has data-lang="fr-FR" and will not be styled by the data-lang|="en" selector.
